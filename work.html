University

MunixTour - A Robot-Guided City Tour

How could a city tour through the city of munich look like when tourists are guided by a robot? During our Design Workshop II. We wanted to answer this question by following a whole design thinking process. We started by creating moodboards that combined the visual style of the city of munich with modern shapes. Based on these moodboards we drew several first design ideas and three user personas that we presented to a focus group. With the help of this focus group we gained insights about user needs, must have and nice to have features, as well as preferences regarding the visual design. The final product of the following design iterations is a video prototype showcasing the city tour of a family of three experiencing munich in an interactive and playful way.

VR Game: Fly me to the mooncake

"Fly me to the mooncake" is a short singleplayer VR Rail-Looter which came to life during a university course. We developed a concept that introduces body motion as player input letting the player navigating an UFO by moving their body. The aim of the game is to rescue astrocats (astronaut cats) that got lost in the milky way and to survive the travel until arriving at a planet in the shape of a mooncake.
