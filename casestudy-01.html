Case Study

MunixTour - A Robot-Guided City Tour

Duration: 3 Months
Year: 2022
Context: Design Workshop at University
The Team: Three HCI students and one Art & Multimedia student
My Role: UX Research & Design

The Problem

How can the City of Munich enhance their tourist services by introducing a robotic interface for city tours that adapts to their tourists' diverse needs and does not interfere with the cityscape?

The Process

After evaluating requirements and user needs, we developed a concept for a robotic interface resulting in a video prototype as deliverable. The process can be structured into four distinguished steps: Discovery, Define, Ideate, and Hand-off.
