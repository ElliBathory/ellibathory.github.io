About me

As research associate at HFF Munich's innovation hub CreatiF Center I am currently responsible for researching technical innovations in film and television.

During my master studies in HCI at LMU Munich, I worked on games, mixed reality, and innovative interaction concepts, among other topics.

At various stages of my professional career, I have gained experience in UX design, web development, and digital twin engineering while working in interdisciplinary, agile teams. Outside of work, I am a singer in a band, which helps me refresh my mind for new ideas.
