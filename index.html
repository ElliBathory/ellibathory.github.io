Elizabeth Bouquet is currently a research associate at the CreatiF Center at the HFF Munich and, together with her team, is responsible for researching technical innovations in film and television.

During her master's degree in Human-Computer Interaction at LMU Munich, she worked on games, mixed reality, and innovative interaction concepts, among other topics. At various stages of her professional career, she has gained extensive experience in web development, user experience design, and digital twin engineering. Outside of work, she is a singer in a band, which helps her clear her mind for new ideas.
